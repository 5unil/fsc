Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

Founder Meets is a private founder community operating in London. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, and your rights over it.

What we collect

  • Information you submit in the application form (such as your name, email, and details about your business), collected via our form provider, Tally.
  • Payment details, collected and processed by our payment provider, Stripe. We do not store your card number.
  • Basic usage analytics (such as page views) via Cloudflare, and the Meta (Facebook) pixel, which uses cookies to help us measure and improve our advertising.
  • One functional cookie that remembers which version of the site you first saw, so we can show you a consistent experience.

How we use it

To review and respond to your application, to match you with other members, to set up and take payment for membership if you are accepted, and to operate and improve the service.

Who we share it with

We share data only with the providers that run the service for us: Tally (forms), Stripe (payments), Cloudflare (hosting and analytics), and Meta (advertising measurement). We do not sell your data.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To do so, contact us at team@foundermeets.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email team@foundermeets.com.
